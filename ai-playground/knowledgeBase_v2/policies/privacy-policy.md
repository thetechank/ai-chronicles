# Privacy Policy

FlightAI values your privacy and ensures all personal data is handled responsibly.

### Information We Collect
- Contact details (name, email, phone)  
- Travel preferences and booking history  
- Payment information (tokenized and encrypted)

### Data Usage
- To improve service recommendations  
- To send flight updates and personalized offers  
- To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions

### Data Protection
- All customer data is encrypted in transit and at rest.  
- FlightAI complies with **GDPR** and **EU-US Data Privacy Framework**.  
- Customers can request data deletion by contacting privacy@flightai.com.

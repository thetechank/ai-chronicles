# Loyalty Program: FlightAI Miles

The **FlightAI Miles** program rewards you for every euro spent.  
Miles can be redeemed for flights, upgrades, or partner services.

### Benefits
- Early boarding and extra baggage allowance  
- Lounge access for Gold and Platinum tiers  
- Personalized offers from AI-driven recommendations  

Join for free via the FlightAI app or website.

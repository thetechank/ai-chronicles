# Ticket Booking Service

The FlightAI Assistant enables instant search, comparison, and booking of flights.  
Bookings can be made in multiple currencies and paid using cards, digital wallets, or FlightAI Miles.

### Key Features
- AI-powered price prediction for optimal booking times  
- Group and family bookings with linked seat preferences  
- Real-time seat map visualization  
- 24-hour hold feature for uncertain travel plans

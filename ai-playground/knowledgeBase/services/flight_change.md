# Flight Change Service

FlightAI customers can easily reschedule flights via the chatbot or mobile app.

- Free change within 24 hours of booking.  
- After that, fare difference and change fee may apply.  
- For Platinum members, **two free changes per year** are included.  

### Disruptions
In case of schedule changes or cancellations, affected customers receive an automatic notification and can rebook using the FlightAI Assistant.

# Refund Policy

Refunds are processed automatically once a cancellation is confirmed.  
FlightAI processes all refunds through the original payment method.

### Refund Timelines
- Credit/Debit Card: 5–10 business days  
- Wallet or FlightAI Miles: Instant  
- Bank Transfer: Up to 14 business days

### Non-Refundable Items
- No-show passengers (not checked in by departure time)  
- Add-on services such as seat upgrades or meals  
- Promotional tickets marked as “non-refundable”

### Exceptions
For medical or bereavement cases, refunds may be approved upon submission of documentation via the FlightAI Help Center.

# Flight Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. FlightAI provides flexible cancellation options for all passengers.

### Within 24 Hours of Booking
- 100% refund for tickets canceled within 24 hours of purchase (if departure is more than 7 days away).

### After 24 Hours
- **Economy Flex:** 75% refund up to 48 hours before departure.  
- **Premium and Business:** 90% refund up to 6 hours before departure.  
- **Basic Saver:** Non-refundable.

# Baggage Policy

FlightAI passengers are allowed **one cabin bag and one personal item** free of charge.

### Cabin Baggage
- Maximum weight: **7 kg (15 lbs)**
- Dimensions: **55 x 35 x 25 cm**
- Must fit in the overhead compartment or under the seat.

### Checked Baggage
- Economy: **1 piece up to 23 kg (50 lbs)**
- Premium: **2 pieces up to 32 kg (70 lbs) each**
- Excess baggage charges apply for overweight or additional bags.

### Special Items
Musical instruments, sports gear, and medical equipment can be checked with prior notice.  
Lithium-ion batteries must be carried in hand baggage only.

**Lost or Damaged Bags:**  
File a report with the FlightAI Assistant or at the airport desk within 24 hours of arrival.

# Check-In Policy

FlightAI offers flexible check-in options.

### Online Check-In
- Available **48 hours to 2 hours** before departure via web or app.  
- Boarding passes can be saved digitally or printed.  
- Seat upgrades and baggage add-ons can be managed during check-in.

### Airport Check-In
- Counters open 3 hours before and close 45 minutes before departure.  
- Premium customers have dedicated fast-track counters.

### Late Check-In
Passengers arriving after the check-in deadline may be denied boarding and marked as *no-show* as per ticket conditions.

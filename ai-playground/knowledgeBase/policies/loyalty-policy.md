# Loyalty Program Policy — FlightAI Miles

FlightAI Miles is our reward system designed for frequent flyers.

### Earning Miles
- Earn 1 Mile for every €1 spent on base fare.  
- Bonus: 20% more miles for Premium and Business travelers.  
- Miles can be earned on partner airlines and hotel bookings.

### Redeeming Miles
- Use miles to pay for flights, upgrades, or lounge access.  
- Miles expire after 24 months of inactivity.  
- Platinum members’ miles never expire.

### Membership Tiers
- **Silver:** 0–10,000 miles  
- **Gold:** 10,001–25,000 miles  
- **Platinum:** 25,001+ miles with exclusive concierge service.
